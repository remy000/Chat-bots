# Beginner Workout Routines

## Full Body Beginner Workout (3x per week)

### Day 1, 2, 3: Full Body Circuit

**Warm-up (5-10 minutes):**
- Light cardio (walking, marching in place)
- Arm circles
- Leg swings
- Bodyweight squats

**Main Workout (20-30 minutes):**

1. **Bodyweight Squats** - 3 sets of 8-12 reps
   - Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
   - Lower down as if sitting in a chair
   - Keep chest up and knees behind toes
   - Targets: Quadriceps, glutes, hamstrings

2. **Push-ups** (modified if needed) - 3 sets of 5-10 reps
   - Start in plank position
   - Lower chest to ground
   - Push back up to starting position
   - Modify on knees if needed
   - Targets: Chest, shoulders, triceps

3. **Plank Hold** - 3 sets of 15-30 seconds
   - Hold straight line from head to heels
   - Engage core muscles
   - Don't let hips sag or pike up
   - Targets: Core, shoulders

4. **Lunges** - 3 sets of 6-8 per leg
   - Step forward into lunge position
   - Lower back knee toward ground
   - Push back to starting position
   - Targets: Quadriceps, glutes, hamstrings

5. **Glute Bridges** - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
   - Lie on back with knees bent
   - Lift hips up squeezing glutes
   - Lower back down slowly
   - Targets: Glutes, hamstrings, core

**Cool-down (5-10 minutes):**
- Gentle stretching
- Deep breathing
- Hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds

## Progression Tips

- Start with lower rep ranges and build up
- Focus on proper form over speed
- Rest 1-2 days between workouts
- Increase reps or sets every 1-2 weeks
- Listen to your body and rest when needed

## Equipment Needed
- None! This is a bodyweight routine
- Optional: Yoga mat for floor exercises
